Why we create Putik?

We believe that there is no greater experience than when the people of God come together in unity and worship Jesus. Sadly, the praise-and-worship preparation of a service usually was taken for granted. Putik (Pujian Untuk Kristus), a christian song lyrics and chords app, will help you, cell groups and churches to prepare the right playlists by providing complete songs directory for your next communion.
